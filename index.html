Stackers!
This is a game to test your reflexes. The bar will go from left to right and back until you press the spacebar to drop the bar. In the bottom row, you can drop the bar wherever you want. In any higher row, you must try to drop the bar directly above where it was in the row below. If some cells of the bar are above open space, those cells will fall off and you will have a smaller bar to use from then on. If you lose all three cells, you lose. As you progress to higher rows, the bar will move faster. The goal is to reach the top row and drop the bar without losing all the cells in your bar.
